About the Classes

Glioma
A malignant tumor originating from glial cells in the brain or spine. Most common primary brain tumor.
Meningioma
Tumor arising from the meninges — the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord. Usually benign.
No Tumor
No tumor detected. The brain scan appears normal with no abnormal growths visible.
Pituitary
Tumor in the pituitary gland at the base of the brain. Usually benign and slow-growing.
